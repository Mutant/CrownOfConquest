Changelog

0.6.6 - 29/08/2010

Features

  • Towns now have mayors. If you raid a town and kill the mayor, you will have the option to have one of your characters become mayor

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions

  • Various minor bug fixes

0.6.5 - 18/07/2010

Features

  • Changed raiding to enter the town's "castle", where you can rob the town's treasury by stealth or force

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions

  • Fixed a bug that could lock a party in combat if the garrison they were fighting fled
  • Recruitment prices now take into account magical or upgraded items that the character owns
  • Sell price of items is now reduced if the item is damaged
  • Shops will no longer sell damaged items
  • Garrisons no longer get XP from quests, or affect the party's movement factor
  • Characters in a garrison that's wiped out will are now deleted
  • Fixed Entangle, which was allowed the target to attack more, instead of not at all
  • Several tweaks to the economy

0.6.4 - 10/07/2010

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions

  • Characters will now attack the same target until they kill it, or a different target is selected
  • It now costs 15 turns to create a garrison
  • Combat now costs 1 turns for every 3 rounds
  • Gold and XP awarded from combat now displayed in daily report email
  • Parties no longer charged for tax if they don't have enough turns to move into a town
  • Characters in garrisons can no longer use the town healer or blacksmith
  • Various other minor bug fixes

0.6.3 - 03/07/2010

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions

  • Allowed zooming of map during combat
  • Items are now 'moved' from a character's equipment to an equipment slot, rather than copied
  • Items can now be unequipped
  • Fixed a bug around selling multiple items
  • Character's stats are now updated immediately when equipping items
  • Fixed a bug that let creatures flee into towns

0.6.2 - 22/06/2010

Features

  • Added Enchanted Items

0.6.1 - 15/05/2010

Features

  • Spells can now be cast offline - options are set on the character's spell tab
  • Parties can now attack garrisons

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions

  • Fixed change password functionality
  • Various minor enhancements and fixes to garrison screens
  • Alert now displayed in messages panel when a garrison you own has been in a battle
  • Fixed display of garrison combat in town news
  • Blacksmiths now 'retire' more often
  • Character's in a garrison can now be given equipment if the party is in the same sector
  • Dropping an item now leaves it in the sector, and it can be picked up later (including by other parties)

0.6.0 - 08/05/2010

Features

  • Added Garrisons, which let you leave characters in a sector to fight monsters

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions

  • Fixed some bugs around fleeing
  • Updated help docs
  • Various other bugs fixes and tweaks

0.5.1 - 25/04/2010

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions

  • Fixed a bug that meant count of killed monsters was sometimes wrong on combat log
  • Dungeons are now only "found" (added to Known Dungeons) once the party enters a sector - not just having the sector on their map
  • Dead characters can no longer attempt to unblock stuck doors
  • Fixed a bug where "not enough gold" message was displaying incorrectly when entering a town
  • New archers now start with more sling stones
  • Time until the next new day is now displayed
  • Various other cosmetic fixes

0.5.0 - 20/04/2010

Features

  • Offline combat - monsters will now attack you in the wilderness, even if you're not logged in. Also, any battles you leave in progress will be finished without you
  • Roads now connect some towns, making movement faster
  • Character's now have a limit of how much equipment they can carry
  • Optimised Dungeon movement - should be significantly faster now
  • Added a new quest type - find an artifact
  • Character equipment screen enhanced

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions

  • Flee message now displays if you flee into a town
  • Parties will no longer be offered the Raid quest if they are too low a level to raid towns
  • Town prestige tweaked - it's now harder to affect
  • An optional email daily report of activity is now sent out to active players
  • Fixed a bug which let you discover more of the map just by zooming out
  • Added treasure chests to dungeons
  • Added various email settings to Party Options
  • Added an option to delete your account from the game completely
  • Added announcements, which can be emailed or viewed when logging in
  • Added Tip of the Day

0.4.1 - 11/07/2009

Features

  • Dungeons now randomly disappear from the map (to be replaced by new ones)
  • Doors in dungeons can now be hidden, stuck, locked or magically sealed
  • Dungeon and main screen map can now be zoomed

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions

  • Lighted area now displayed in dungeons
  • Multiple items can now be sold at once
  • Battles in dungeons now displayed in party's combat log, as well as location of the battle

0.4.0 - 29/06/2009

Features

  • Town prosperity will now change daily as a result of events around the town
  • Towns can now be raided for gold (once a party reaches a minimum level - currently 8)
  • Parties now have a 'prestige' rating with a town, affected by several factors. Towns may offer discounts to parties with good prestige

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions

  • New parties now start in a town with a minimum prosperity rating
  • Creatures now have different display names for their weapons
  • Completed Quests are now reported in the news (along with raids and prosperity changes)
  • Added a quest type to raid a town
  • Several other misc. bug fixes

0.3.2 - 17/05/2009

Features

  • New Mage Spell - "Watcher"
  • New parties get a "Watcher" effect for their first 20 days
  • Characters can now be female

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions

  • Fixed a bug that was causing creature's AF/DF to be calculated incorrectly (making them weaker)
  • Added a warning when a character runs out of ammo for their equipped weapon
  • Other parties in sector now display in town
  • Added rounds left to effects in combat. Creature effects are now also displayed in the round they're cast in
  • Fixed a bug that caused the same type of creatures to be added to a group more than once, messing up the numbering
  • Fixed a bug that was letting low level monsters have rare items

Bug Fixes & Minor Additions
