New Day

Every 24 hours, a new day will be started within the game. In your party, your spell-casters' spells will also be re-memorised, and if your party is rested, they will heal. Many magical items give benefits each day (such as more turns or healing).

Each day, there are also many other changes around the Kingdom's world. Town prosperity may go up or down, mayor approval ratings can change, elections in towns are run, etc.
