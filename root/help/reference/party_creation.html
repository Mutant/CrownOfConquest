Party Creation

Before you start playing, you'll need to create your party. First, choose a name for your party, and click "Add Character". This will take you to the new character screen.

Enter a name for the character, and choose the character's race and class (see below for descriptions of these). You can then adjust your character's stats by typing numbers into the 'Modified' field. As you add stat points, you'll notice the "Points Remaining" is reduced. A new character can have a maximum of [% c.config.stat_max %] for a particular stat.

Once you've created [% c.config.new_party_characters %] characters, click the "Finish Party Setup" to start playing the game.

Stats

The following describes each of the stats. Note, each stat affects many aspects of the game. Only the most important ones are mentioned below.

  • Strength: Physical Power. Affects combat with melee weapons.
  • Agility: Dexterity. Affects combat with ranged weapons.
  • Intelligence: Smarts. Affects spell casting for Mages.
  • Divinity: Favour with the gods, and general luck. Affects spell casting for Priests.
  • Consitution: Physical Fitness. Affects hit points, and how far a character can move in a game turn.

Races

Races primarily affect the stats of a character. Each race has a different base value for each stat.

Classes

The following classes are available.

  • Warrior: Favours combat with melee weapons.
  • Archer: Favours combat with ranged weapons.
  • Priest: Can cast spells that primarily enhance characters or the whole party.
  • Mage: Can cast spells that primarily hinder or damage opponents.
