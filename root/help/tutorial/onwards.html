Onwards!

That's the end of this guide, but it's by no means the end of your journey in the Crown Of Conquest world. In fact, we've barely scratched the surface. A few of the things you might want to do as your party levels up:

  • Find a dungeon, where you'll find many more monsters to battle, not to mention treasure (and much more!)
  • Create a garrison with some of your characters to earn gold and experience
  • Become mayor of a town, through democratic means, or by force
  • Construct towers, forts or castles

You can find more information in the reference guide, or better yet - discover the game by playing it!

Good luck, and if you find yourself really stuck, head to the forum for help.
