Disband Party

So you want to disband your party..? This is an irreversible action! Your characters will disperse across the countryside, take up jobs as farmers, blacksmiths or village idiots, and never be heard from again.

If you're really sure you want to do this, click the confirmation button below. Once you've disbanded this party, you'll be allowed to create a new one.

Just to be 100% clear, once you click the button below, THERE'S NO GOING BACK!
